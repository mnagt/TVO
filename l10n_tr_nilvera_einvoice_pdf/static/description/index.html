Effortless e-Invoice PDF Integration for Turkish Businesses.

Seamless e-Invoice PDF Integration into Odoo Chatter and E-mail template.

Keep your e-Invoice PDFs organized and accessible with zero effort.

Perfect for companies operating in Turkey that need to integrate e-Invoice PDF retrieval into their Odoo workflow.

This module automatically retrieves e-Invoice PDFs from an external API for Turkish companies and attaches them directly to the related record's chatter in Odoo, and it can also attach it to predesigned e-mail template. It simplifies document management, ensuring all invoices are securely stored and easily accessible within the system.
